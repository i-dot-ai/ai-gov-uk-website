What are you looking to do with AI?

These resources have been shared by the public sector community. You can email ai-knowledge-hub@dsit.gov.uk to share your tools or use our form to share your prompts.

  • Find information

    Answer questions using chatbots and semantic search

  • Analyse data and text

    Review text and data to find themes, check standards and predict outcomes

  • Collect and clean data

    Gather data and organise it so it's accurate, consistent and ready to use

  • Create and review documents

    Write reports and emails, create strategies and summarise information

  • Triage and route information

    Review and sort information, and send requests to the right place

  • Run meetings and appointments

    Support meetings and appointments by transcribing, translating and preparing content

  • Digitise documents

    Turn paper documents and images into structured digital data that you can search, analyse and share
