This register maps the 13 official government functions to operational areas that can be transformed by AI and identifies the COO lead responsible for each area.

To update ownership details: Contact saasha.nair@dsit.gov.uk

Note: Policy and Operational Delivery are included as non-functional categories representing high-impact cross-government operational activities.
